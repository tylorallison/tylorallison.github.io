Singed Dirt

Posted on Wed 02 August 2017 in games

The Game

A modern 3d take on the classic Scorched Earth game supporting local hot seat, matchmaker and local lan multiplayer.

Enter the arena to vent your frustrations by dominating your opponents. Or just have fun with the characters and explore our unique game play features. Watch out for the big boom!

My Contribution

Network code, lobby UI and code, items pickups, chat, turns and ammo UI, voronoi terrain and procedural spawn locations, player customization menu.

Play

This game was developed by members of the Gamkedo club, myself included. Find out more about the club, or play this game: Play @ Gamkedo.
